## Build Devices That Keep Mattering

It is easy to build a device, deploy it, and lose track of it. A year on, no one is quite sure whether it still works or whether it ever earned its keep. Monitoring changes that. When a device reports to siliconwit.io from the start, it stops being a black box: you can watch it do its job, improve it on evidence rather than guesswork, and learn early whether it was a sound investment or one to retire. A device you can see is one you can keep making better; a device you cannot see is one you will eventually forget.

## Try It in Five Minutes

You do not need hardware, or even an account, to start.

<Steps>

1. **Try it without signing up** at [siliconwit.io/try](https://siliconwit.io/try), where you can see live data and the dashboards before creating an account.

2. **Check the protocols** at [mqtt.siliconwit.io](https://mqtt.siliconwit.io), the protocols home page that lists the MQTT, MQTT-SN, CoAP, and HTTP endpoints and how to connect to each.

3. **Flash a real board** using a pre-built firmware image for common boards (ESP32, Raspberry Pi), with credentials embedded so you are not editing C on day one.

4. **Watch it appear** on your dashboard, set a rule, and, if you like, opt the device into the public dashboard as a live showcase.

</Steps>

## Security and Access

- TLS encryption on every protocol, with per-device tokens.
- Account protection with 2FA and passkeys.
- You control which of your devices, if any, are public.

## Hardware and Documentation

The [hardware store](https://store.siliconwit.io) ships pre-tested dev boards and starter kits (the fastest way to a working device), and the full platform [documentation](https://docs.siliconwit.io) covers recipes, SDKs, and protocol references.

## Pricing

Plans scale with the number of devices you run: a **Free** tier to start, then **Starter**, **Business**, **Scale**, and **Enterprise**, with the higher tiers adding more devices, the AI tier, and read/write MCP access. You pay for the devices you actually run, and you can begin at no cost. See [siliconwit.io](https://siliconwit.io) for current pricing.
